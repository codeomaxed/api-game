# Monster Prompt Variants

Three complete sets of dungeon prompts with different monster variants, maintaining the exact same structure and style as the original "Pale Cave Spider" prompts.

---

## Variant 1: Wraith (Spectral Undead)

### 0-0-0: DEAD END (No Exits)
Grimdark fantasy masterpiece, heavily inspired by Dark Souls 3. A cinematic 16:9 wide-angle shot of a sealed dungeon chamber. The camera looks straight at a flat, blank stone wall. Crouching small in the middle distance of the vast floor is a Wraith facing the camera (Front View). The creature has a translucent, ethereal form that flickers like smoke, with hollow eye sockets glowing with pale blue fire and tattered spectral robes that drift in an unseen wind. The back wall (center) is a continuous, seamless expanse of heavy wet bricks from floor to ceiling. The left wall is a solid, blank surface of wet masonry. The right wall is a solid, blank surface of wet masonry. The floor is a wide expanse of broken wet stone reflecting faint orange torchlight. Heavy vignette, pitch-black shadows, filthy textures, thick fog, claustrophobic, 8k resolution, photorealistic grim concept art.
